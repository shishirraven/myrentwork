Privacy Policy for My Rent Work App

This Privacy Policy outlines how My Rent Work (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information gathered from users (“you” or “your”) of the My Rent Work mobile application.

Information We Collect:

  1. Personal Information:
    • We may collect personal information such as names, addresses, and contact details when you register or use our app.
  2. Usage Information:
    • We collect non-personal information about how you interact with our app, including usage patterns and preferences.

How We Use Collected Information:

  1. Rent Calculation and Billing:
    • Personal information is used to calculate rent, generate bills, and manage utility expenses according to your preferences.
  2. Access Control:
    • We may use information to provide access control features, allowing you to share property details with designated individuals.
  3. Improving User Experience:
    • Non-personal information is utilized to understand user behavior and enhance the functionality and user experience of the app.

Data Security:

  • We prioritize the security of your information and employ industry-standard measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

Third-Party Access:

  • Your information is not shared with third parties unless necessary for app functionality or required by law.

Your Choices:

  • You can choose not to provide certain information, but this may limit the features and functionality of the app.

Changes to this Privacy Policy:

  • We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Users are encouraged to check this page regularly for any changes.

Contact Us:

By using the My Rent Work app, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
